# B2B Lead Generation Tool for Marketers & Agencies
Automated tool to extract business emails for lead generation and outreach

Generate targeted business leads and emails in seconds for outreach, ads, and client acquisition.
---

##  Problem

Finding business emails manually is slow, inconsistent, and limits scaling for agencies and freelancers.

Most marketers waste hours collecting leads instead of focusing on conversions.

---

##  Solution

This tool automates the process of extracting business emails based on your target niche and location.

You can quickly generate a list of potential leads for:

* Cold outreach
* Meta Ads services
* Local business marketing
* Freelance client acquisition

---

##  How It Works

1. Enter your target:

   * Example: `gyms in Delhi`
2. The tool processes the query
3. Extracts relevant business data
4. Outputs structured lead information

---

##  Example

**Input:**
gyms in Delhi

**Output:**

| Business Name  | Email                                                   | Location |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- | -------- |
| FitZone Gym    | [info@fitzone.com](mailto:info@fitzone.com)             | Delhi    |
| PowerHouse Gym | [contact@powerhouse.com](mailto:contact@powerhouse.com) | Delhi    |
